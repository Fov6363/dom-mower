Field notes / 14 July

A build is made from browser commands.

Instead of collecting generic damage bonuses, you assemble a moveset from familiar browser actions and CSS rules.

Find a pattern. Cut it from the page. Paste the fragments back as a weapon.
1 / ⌘F

Find

Chains damage through related DOM elements.

2 / ⌘X

Cut

Collects nearby targets in your clipboard.

3 / ⌘V

Paste

Converts saved fragments into explosive attacks.

Your upgrade path

a → img → .class → :nth-child → * → !important

Selectors change which enemies your commands can reach. Combinators change how damage spreads through the page.
